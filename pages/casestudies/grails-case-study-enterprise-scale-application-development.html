Case Study Enterprise-Scale

THE RIGHT SOFTWARE AND SUPPORT TEAM FOR ENTERPRISE-SCALE APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT

The Grails® framework and core development team deliver the tools and support an enterprise company needs to overhaul its technology stack and enhance operations across the board.

THE OBJECTIVE

A leader in the global life reinsurance industry set out to upgrade its systems to support:

  • Increased demands for speed-to-market
  • Continuous innovation
  • Scalability
  • Reduced software licensing costs

The solution

The company identified the Grails framework as the ideal toolkit for the project. Systems were successfully upgraded using the Grails framework as the driver for their:

  • Internal and external web services
  • XML SOAP-based services
  • REST services
  • AngularJS applications
  • Traditional web 1.0 applications

THE RIGHT TECHNOLOGY

Outstanding software engineering support and strong documentation, bolstered by a vibrant user community, make the Grails framework a terrific choice for any organization looking to quickly bring industrial-strength, scalable, and integrated solutions to market.

Because the Grails framework represents a combination of convention over configuration and sensible defaults, it has been described as “Java on steroids.”

Popular among shops that have invested in Java or JVM-based technologies, the Grails framework:

  • Facilitates rapid application development
  • Is welcomed by development teams, including those currently using Spring and Hibernate, due to its ability to accelerate developer productivity by 50-70%
  • Is applauded by businesses as a tailored feature set, resulting in streamlined learning, development efficiency, and support responsiveness
  • Supports exponential scaling
  • Deploys well within existing, complex environments
  • Provides necessary flexibility to develop plug-ins and to quickly resolve issues

Grails is not just for rapid prototyping; our situation is proof that Grails is built for enterprise-scale application development.

Development with Grails is significantly faster than with Java, or anything else we had worked with or explored.

OCI consultants deliver a level of consistency that we haven’t seen. And that consistency means reduced risk to our projects.
