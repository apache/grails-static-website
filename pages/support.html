The Grails® Foundation is committed to the continued innovation, development, maintenance, and support of the Grails framework. Our goal is to provide Grails framework users the support they need while maintaining as much focus as possible on innovation. The level and type of support available for earlier versions of the Grails framework will change as the framework continues to evolve.

For information about the currently supported versions of the Grails framework, please see our support schedule.

The Grails Foundation offers flexible, customizable open source support services with direct access to the architects and engineers who develop the Grails framework and have spent their careers supporting and maturing it.

We can assist you with:

  • Architecture and design review
  • Rapid prototyping, troubleshooting, and debugging
  • Customizing, modifying, and extending the product
  • Integration assistance and support
  • Mentorship and architecture throughout the lifecycle of your project
