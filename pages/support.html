Premium Product Support

Starting at $1,500 / month

As the Grails framework grows and evolves, it also changes. Some of these changes may not be compatible with older versions of the framework. While the updates may be more ideal for the future, it may also make it more difficult for organizations to easily upgrade their Grails framework applications to the latest version. For this reason, we are proud to offer premium product support for many versions of the Grails framework, including versions that have reached the end of public support. For more information about premium support options for your Grails framework applications, please see our support schedule.

Our premium support packages include:

  • Resolution or mitigation options for critical and high security vulnerabilities
  • 120 Ad-Hoc support hours
  • 3 seats to public Grails Foundation training events with a 30% discount on additional seats purchased
  • Access to premium-member prizes, contests, and events

Please contact us for more information. Our team is happy to work with you and identify the right premium support package for your needs.

Subscription-Based Support

Starting at $1,350 / month
Hours expire at the end of each month

Getting expert help with your Grails framework applications is an email away with our subscription support packages. With subscription-based support, your organization is guaranteed a number of support hours each month. Use your hours to get assistance directly from the team that builds and maintains the Grails framework.

Our subscription-based support packages include:

  • A minimum of 10 Ad-Hoc support hours each month
  • 30% discount for seats to public Grails Foundation training events
  • Access to premium-member prizes, contests, and events
