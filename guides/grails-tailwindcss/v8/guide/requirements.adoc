To complete this guide you will need:

* JDK 21
* link:https://nodejs.org[Node.js] 20+ and a recent npm (the Tailwind 4 CLI is published as `@tailwindcss/cli` on npm; no other build tooling is required)
* A Bash, PowerShell, or `cmd` shell capable of running the included Gradle wrapper (`./gradlew` or `gradlew.bat`)
* About 30 minutes
