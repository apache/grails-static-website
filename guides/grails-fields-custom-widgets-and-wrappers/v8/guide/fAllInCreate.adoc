Three things to notice:

. The body of the form is a single `<f:all bean="book"/>` line.
. `except="dateCreated,lastUpdated"` skips audit columns the user has no business editing - the Fields plugin already excludes `id`, `version`, `errors`, and Hibernate's `belongsTo` back-references by default.
. Validation feedback is handled by `<g:hasErrors>` once at the top of the form - the per-field error display is the wrapper template's responsibility, which we will customise later.
